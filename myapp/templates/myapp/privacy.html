Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to AgroBrain . At AgroBrain, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and agricultural data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and farm details when you create an account or contact us.
  • Agricultural Data: Farm location, soil analysis results, crop types, yield data, and other farming-related information you input or that our systems generate.
  • Weather Data: Local weather information related to your farm's location.
  • Device & Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and how you interact with our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide personalized agricultural insights and recommendations.
  • Generate accurate weather forecasts for your farm location.
  • Analyze soil quality and suggest optimal crop management strategies.
  • Offer market analysis to help you make informed decisions about crop sales.
  • Improve our platform and develop new features based on user needs.
  • Communicate important updates and agricultural tips.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or agricultural data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate our platform, such as cloud storage providers and analytics services.
  • Agricultural Partners: With your explicit consent, we may share anonymized data with research institutions or agricultural organizations to improve farming practices.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal and agricultural data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.
  • Choose what agricultural data you share with our platform.

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related queries, please contact us at privacy@agrobrain.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through our platform.
